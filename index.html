"Ah... Amaterasu. Origin of all that is good and mother to us all..."

Take your part in this epic tale as the Sun Goddess, Amaterasu. She takes the form of a wolf as she travels around the country of Nippon, based on ancient Japan as told by mythology spawned from the time!

Change the land around you with your celestial powers. It's as simple as drawing on the paper! With them, the power of the sun, the moon, water, life, slashes and even more are on your side against your enemies!

However, the forces of the evil eight-headed Orochi won't let your journey be simple... So crush them using Amaterasu's Godly weapons - Swords, Rosary Beads, and Divine Shields - with the aid of your Celestial Brush and the wandering artist Issun!
